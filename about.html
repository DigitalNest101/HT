Our Story

Our firm was incorporated in the year 2009 as a Partnership entity at Balanagar in Hyderabad. The partner, Mr. Parashuram has excellent entrepreneur skills of running the business. He has remained totally devoted towards all the business activities right from purchase of Raw Materials to fabrication of products and to final finish till delivery. The product range Sheet metal fabrications using the ALUMINUM, CRC, GI, SS SHEETS. latest technology with well equipped infrastructure for any type of manufacturing. ELECTRONIC & ELECTRICAL ENCLOSURE, FIRE BOXES, PRESSED STEEL DOORS WINDOW FRAMES. Factory Establishd in The 2009-made under strict supervision in our fully equipped industrial unit situated at Hydarabad.. The firm has employed technical professionals to ensure effective steps have taken to maintain the quality at all stages of production and to take care of perfection of products fabricated for the full satisfaction of the clients. The competence of our personnel is backed up by many years of experience in the profession.

WHAT WE PROVIDE

The firm provides entire products range to the clients at very competitive and affordable prices and as well as ensures not to make any compromise with quality of raw materials used or for joints or for final paint finish of products. Therefore, the products find acceptance on top priority. Various different sections have been set up for different operations like metal sheet straightening, cutting, molding and shaping and for other different operations in stages of manufacturing, assembling and finishing. Quality Control is one major aspect that is taken care of very vigilantly so that there is no lapse in the quality that the firm intends to deliver. The close coordination of each and every workman in various sections and their cordial relationship creates an atmosphere of smooth functioning resulting in utmost productivity and growth of firm.
